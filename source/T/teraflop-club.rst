.. _teraflop-club:

============================================================
teraflop club
============================================================

/te´r\@·flop kluhb/, n\.

[FLOP = Floating Point Operation] A mythical association of people who consume outrageous amounts of computer time in order to produce a few simple pictures of glass balls with intricate ray-tracing techniques.
Caltech professor James Kajiya is said to have been the founder.
Compare :ref:`Knights-of-the-Lambda-Calculus`\.
