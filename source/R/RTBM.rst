============================================================
RTBM
============================================================

/R·T·B·M/, imp\.

[Unix] Commonwealth Hackish variant of :ref:`RTFM`\; expands to ‘Read The Bloody Manual’.
RTBM is often the entire text of the first reply to a question from a :ref:`newbie`\; the *second* would escalate to "RTFM".
