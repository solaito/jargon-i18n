.. _octal-forty:

============================================================
octal forty
============================================================

/ok´tl for´tee/, n\.

Hackish way of saying "I'm drawing a blank."
Octal 40 is the :ref:`ASCII` space character, 0100000; by an odd coincidence, :ref:`hex` 40 (01000000) is the :ref:`EBCDIC` space character.
See :ref:`wall`\.
