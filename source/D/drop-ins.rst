.. _drop-ins:

============================================================
drop-ins
============================================================

n\.

[prob.
: by analogy with :ref:`drop-outs`\] Spurious characters appearing on a terminal or console as a result of line noise or a system malfunction of some sort.
Esp.
: used when these are interspersed with one's own typed input.
Compare :ref:`drop-outs`\, sense 2.
