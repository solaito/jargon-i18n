.. _dumbass-attack:

============================================================
dumbass attack
============================================================

/duhm´as \@·tak´/, n\.

[Purdue] Notional cause of a novice's mistake made by the experienced, especially one made while running as :ref:`root` under Unix, e.g., typing :code:`rm -r \*` or :code:`mkfs` on a mounted file system.
Compare :ref:`adger`\.
