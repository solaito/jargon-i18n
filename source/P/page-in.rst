============================================================
page in
============================================================

v\.

[MIT]

1.
   To become aware of one's surroundings again after having paged out (see :ref:`page-out`\).
   Usually confined to the sarcastic comment: "Eric pages in, :ref:`film-at-11`\!"

2.
   Syn.
   swap in; see :ref:`swap`\.
