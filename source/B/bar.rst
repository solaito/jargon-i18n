============================================================
bar
============================================================

/bar/, n\.

1.
   [very common] The second :ref:`metasyntactic-variable`\, after :ref:`foo` and before :ref:`baz`\.
   "Suppose we have two functions: FOO and BAR.
   FOO calls BAR...."

2.
   Often appended to :ref:`foo` to produce :ref:`foobar`\.
