.. _Bohr-bug:

============================================================
Bohr bug
============================================================

/bohr buhg/, n\.

[from quantum physics] A repeatable :ref:`bug`\; one that manifests reliably under a possibly unknown but well-defined set of conditions.
Antonym of :ref:`heisenbug`\; see also :ref:`mandelbug`\, :ref:`schroedinbug`\.
