.. _bytesexual:

============================================================
bytesexual
============================================================

/bi:t\`sek´shu·\@l/, adj\.

[rare] Said of hardware, denotes willingness to compute or pass data in either :ref:`big-endian` or :ref:`little-endian` format (depending, presumably, on a :ref:`mode-bit` somewhere).
See also :ref:`NUXI-problem`\.
