.. _flamage:

============================================================
flamage
============================================================

/flay'm\@j/, n\.

[very common] Flaming verbiage, esp.
high-noise, low-signal postings to :ref:`Usenet` or other electronic :ref:`fora`\.
Often in the phrase the usual flamage.
Flaming is the act itself; flamage the content; a flame is a single flaming message.
See :ref:`flame`\, also :ref:`dahmum`\.
